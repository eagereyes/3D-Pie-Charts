Part-Whole Charts Study

Please read these instructions carefully before continuing!

This study examines how well people can read area in charts that represent a value using area. Some of these charts will look like pie charts, most will be a bit unusual though.

Your task is to read the value that is shown in the blue (darker) part of each chart as a percentage. Regardless of the shape of the blue part, estimate the area covered by it as a percentage of the entire circle.

Please answer as accurately as possible, but don't dwell on individual charts. If your accuracy is considerably worse than the average, we will not be able to use the data and won't approve the HIT.

The complete study should take about 15-20 minutes. You will be given a chance to rest after one third and two thirds of the questions. There will be a progress bar at the bottom showing you where tou are in the study.

In most browsers, you can enter a number into the answer field and then hit Return or Enter to advance to the next question. That is the faster way, but you can also click the Next button (especially if you're using Internet Explorer).

At the very end, you will see a code. Please copy and paste that code into the input field on the Mechanical Turk website.

We really appreciate your help with this study. Thank you!
